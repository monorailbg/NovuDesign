Who you're working with

One person.
Total ownership.

My name is Immanuel Hoehne. I'm a web designer based in Japan, working with local businesses in Germany, Australia, and beyond. I started Novu Design because I kept seeing the same problem: excellent small businesses — dentists, salons, restaurants, therapists — running on websites that made them look like they'd closed in 2012.

Every project comes through me directly. I handle the strategy, the design, and the build. No account managers, no outsourced developers, no handoffs. When you email me, I reply. When something needs fixing, I fix it. That's the whole model.

I use the latest generation of AI tooling to move faster — but every site is finished by hand, and nothing ships unless I'm proud of it.
