Our team

Meet the team.

Atelier Wolke was founded by Mia Wolke in 2018 after years training in Hamburg and Berlin. The vision: a calm, unhurried salon where the focus is entirely on you — your hair, your lifestyle, your result.

Our team of four stylists each specialises in a different area: colour, cuts, extensions, and treatment. Every appointment begins with a proper consultation.
