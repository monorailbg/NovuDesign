Our story

Started with one
perfect cup.

Kaoru was born from a two-year trip through Japan's coffee culture — small kissaten in Kyoto, third-wave bars in Tokyo. We came home to Leipzig with one question: why can't we have this here?

Everything we serve is chosen for flavour, provenance, and craft. We roast weekly with our partner in Saxony and source directly from three farms we've visited in person.
