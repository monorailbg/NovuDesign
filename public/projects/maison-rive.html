Our story

Born from a love of real food.

Maison Rive opened in 2019 with one conviction: that a great meal is less about the number of courses and more about the honesty of each one. Chef Elias Brandt spent six years in kitchens across Lyon, Copenhagen, and Berlin before returning to Leipzig to open something of his own.

Every dish starts with a question: what's extraordinary right now? The answer changes weekly.
