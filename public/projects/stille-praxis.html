Psychotherapy · Leipzig

A space to think.
A space to heal.

Individual psychotherapy for adults navigating anxiety, burnout, life transitions, and the quieter struggles that are hardest to name. Sessions available in German and English.

First sessions are 60 minutes and focus entirely on getting to know you — no pressure, no agenda. Sessions available in person and online.

How I work

A grounded approach

My practice draws from cognitive behavioural therapy, acceptance and commitment therapy, and depth psychology — used flexibly, depending on what you need.

01

Evidence-based

Rooted in methods with strong research support. We work with what's proven, not trends.

02

Person-centred

No two people are the same. Your therapy is built around your life, values, and pace.

03

Long-term focus

We're not here to patch symptoms. We work toward lasting change and self-understanding.

Areas of support

What we work on

You don't need a diagnosis to seek support. If something is weighing on you, that's enough.

Anxiety & worry

Generalised anxiety, social anxiety, panic attacks, health anxiety.

Burnout & exhaustion

Work-related stress, emotional depletion, loss of motivation and meaning.

Depression & low mood

Persistent sadness, emptiness, difficulty experiencing joy or connection.

Life transitions

Relocation, career change, relationship endings, identity shifts.

Relationship difficulties

Communication, conflict, attachment patterns, and trust issues.

Self-esteem & identity

Inner criticism, people-pleasing, perfectionism, and self-worth.
